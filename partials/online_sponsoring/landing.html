FAQs

Q. What does it mean to be a JAFRA Consultant?

A. You’re out there as our representative selling JAFRA’s excellent products directly to customers at hosted parties, casual get-togethers wherever you like or online. It’s up to you!

Q. How do I begin to share JAFRA products?

A. Simply make a list of your friends, family, and neighbors, and give them a call. Let them know you’re now a JAFRA Consultant, and that you want to show them the beauty of JAFRA products.

Q. How much can I expect to earn?

A. You’re the CEO of your business so you decide! You can earn up to 50% personal commissions on sales. The more you sell, the more you’ll earn!

Q. How will I get paid?

A. You have two options. Payments are made: 1) Via direct deposit into your checking account 2) Loaded onto a JAFRA Debit MasterCard.

Q. Will I receive training from JAFRA?

A. Yes! In no time, you’ll be a JAFRA expert with our convenient and powerful online training. Your JAFRA Manager can also help you learn.

Q. Do I need to work a certain amount of hours?

A. No to that. You’re the boss so you decide how much time you spend on your JAFRA business.

Q. Do I need to achieve a minimum number of sales?

A. We have a rule for this one: To remain a JAFRA Consultant, you are required to place at least $300 or more in retail orders in a 12-month period.

Q. How much do I have to invest?

A. You can purchase our Royal Starter Kit for $99 plus shipping and handling. It has everything you need to succeed. Look for Quick Start kits at a lower price.

Q. Does JAFRA offer a product guarantee?

A. Of course! We offer free, no-hassle returns and exchanges within 6 months of a product’s purchase date.

Q. How is JAFRA different from other direct sale cosmetic companies?

A. Founded in 1956 in Malibu, California, JAFRA has over 58 years in helping women achieve their dreams. Over 500,000 Consultants sell our products in 20 countries. Plus, JAFRA is ranked in the top 25 direct sales companies in the United States.
