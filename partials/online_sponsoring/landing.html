OUR PRODUCTS

PURITY + PERFORMANCE

The finest ingredients, blended and formulated through a state-of-the-art, cruelty-free process. Samples are taken at every step to test purity, effectiveness, and performance. This passion for quality and integrity in our products has proven to keep customers coming back for more.

SKINCARE

Our skin care heritage began with Royal Jelly, an all-natural ingredient super-rich in proteins and vitamins. Today, it is featured in all our legendary Royal Jelly skin care treatments. We are also known for innovative skin care such as Advanced Dynamics regimens and JAFRA PRO cosmeceuticals.

FRAGRANCE

World-renowned European perfumers develop JAFRA scents with superior ingredients. They also understand a fragrance must create memories, evoke moments in time, and reflect our personality. We offer extensive lines for women and men of all ages, even babies.

COLOR

When you see JAFRA color, you’ll see why our products are on top of trends and fashionably inspired. Choose from flawless-look foundations, full coverage lipsticks in luscious colors, high fashion eyeshadows and eyeliners, and must-have nail lacquers.
