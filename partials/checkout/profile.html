JAFRA Independent Consultant Agreement

JAFRA Cosmetics International, Inc. (“JAFRA”), with its principal place of business located at 2451 Townsgate Road, Westlake Village, CA 91361, agrees to sell to the Independent JAFRA Consultant, who has consented to and entered this Agreement (“Consultant”), its line of cosmetics and other products; and the Consultant agrees to purchase such cosmetics and other products from JAFRA under the terms and conditions of this Agreement.

1. Consultant Kit. The Consultant submits full payment for the Consultant Kit to JAFRA upon execution and submission of this Agreement to JAFRA. The Consultant may return the Consultant Kit and its undisturbed contents within 30 days for a full refund by submitting a signed written request to JAFRA for return authorization. Consultant Kit refunds will be made directly to the Consultant. In the event the Consultant returns the Consultant Kit, this Agreement is terminated.

2. Payment Terms. The Consultant agrees to pay JAFRA for the merchandise in accordance with JAFRA’s payment terms and credit policy as published and amended from time to time. JAFRA shall have no obligation to fulfill any future orders should the Consultant fail to pay for any previous orders or if there is any outstanding amount due to JAFRA. In addition, JAFRA shall have the right to withhold the payment of all or part of any discount, commission, awards, incentives or other monies due to the Consultant and/or the right to set off any monies that may be due or payable to JAFRA by the Consultant against any such payment of discount, commission, awards, incentives or other monies due to the Consultant. The Consultant agrees to be liable to JAFRA for any additional costs incurred recovering any overdue amount from the Consultant including attorney fees and court costs incurred by JAFRA.

3. Buyback Policy. If this Agreement is terminated at any time, JAFRA will repurchase currently marketable inventory at 90% of the Consultant’s original net cost less appropriate set-offs and legal claims, if any. All other product returns shall be governed by JAFRA’s general product return policy currently in effect, which policy may be amended from time to time.

4. Method of Sale. The Consultant recognizes the importance of putting forth best efforts in servicing clients and in creating and maintaining the goodwill of clients toward JAFRA. It is understood that JAFRA products are to be sold through the Consultant only to the ultimate consumer. The Consultant agrees that JAFRA products will not be sold or marketed in any retail environment, swap meets, “eBay” or other electronic forums, Farmers Market or website not authorized by JAFRA. The Consultant further agrees that at any demonstration or sales meeting where JAFRA products, JAFRA program or career opportunities are presented, no other products, opportunities, programs or services will be presented or offered. The Consultant agrees to make only those product claims that are specifically set out for each product in materials supplied by JAFRA.

5. Incorporation by Reference. This Agreement, the information herein, JAFRA’s Compensation Plan online document, the JAFRA Independent Consultant Policies and Procedures , online terms and conditions and all related polices including updates and amendments thereto contain and constitute the entire Agreement between the parties, and supersede and terminate all prior agreements and arrangements, if any, whether written, oral, or implied between JAFRA and the Consultant.

6. No Authority. The Consultant acknowledges that the Consultant has no authority to bind or obligate JAFRA in any way.

7. Independence. The Consultant is an independent contractor and not a JAFRA employee for any purpose, including, but not limited to benefits and relevant taxes. The Consultant shall not have the power to represent or bind JAFRA in any way. EACH OF THE PARTIES SHALL ACT IN ALL MATTERS RELATING TO THIS AGREEMENT AS AN INDEPENDENT PARTY AND NOTHING HEREIN SHALL CONSTITUTE ANY OF THEM AS AN AGENT OR PRINCIPAL OR PARTNER OR EMPLOYEE OF THE OTHER PARTY. THE RELATIONSHIP BETWEEN THE CONSULTANT AND JAFRA SHALL NOT BE CONSIDERED TO BE A RELATIONSHIP BETWEEN AN AGENT AND A PRINCIPAL OR A PARTNERSHIP.

8. Recognition. From time to time, JAFRA will be recognizing Consultants, their sales results and their earnings in JAFRA publications. If the Consultant does not want this information published, please notify JAFRA in writing, at the above address.

9. Compensation. Consultant may receive from JAFRA compensation, recognition and benefits as set forth in the “Compensation Plan” online document. Any compensation, recognition or benefits earned will be conditioned upon compliance with the terms of the Compensation Plan, this Agreement, the JAFRA Independent Consultant Policies and Procedures, maintenance of good credit standing with JAFRA, and other applicable JAFRA policies and procedures as they now exist or may be modified in the future at JAFRA’s sole discretion. The standards and criteria for participation in the Compensation Plan as well as entitlement to and formula for, payment of compensation may be modified in JAFRA’s sole discretion with, any such modification becoming effective immediately upon publication of such modification in the “Compensation Plan” online document on the internet at www.jafrabiz.com.
